# Founder Interview Script

For the top-10 outreach calls. Two jobs: (1) **verify and deepen** the public
signals that put them on the list, and (2) **invite** them into the Kuzana
network. Keep it ~30–40 minutes. Listen more than you talk — these are busy
operators, not pitch-deck founders.

## Before the call
- Read their profile (`output/profiles/<slug>.md`) and have the evidence open.
- Note the 2–3 signals that earned their rank and the gaps marked _needs research_.
- Confirm logging owner + that the [outreach tracker](../discovery/outreach.py) row exists.

## Opening (2 min)
> "Thanks for the time. We run Kuzana — we've been mapping Kenyan businesses with
> genuinely strong fundamentals that the startup-media world has somehow missed,
> and yours stood out. This isn't a sales call; I'd love to understand how you've
> built what you've built, and share what we're putting together."

State plainly: no obligation, we'll share their profile before anything is published.

## Section A — Story & founder background (8 min)
1. How did the business start — what were you doing before?
2. What did the first year look like? What nearly killed it early on?
3. Who are the people behind it today — founders, key operators?
4. What's kept you going when it would've been easier to stop?

## Section B — Business model (8 min)
5. In plain terms, how do you make money — who pays, for what?
6. Who are your customers, and how do they find you?
7. What do you do that competitors don't, or won't?
8. What does a normal month look like operationally?

## Section C — Verify the signals (8 min)
*Map each to what the pipeline observed; confirm or correct.*
9. How long have you been operating? (verify `longevity_years`)
10. Roughly how many customers / orders in a typical month? (verify scale)
11. How many locations / branches / staff today, and how has that changed? (growth)
12. Any certifications, standards, or association memberships? (validation)
13. *(if applicable)* We saw you've won public tenders — how big a part of the business is that?
14. What's grown fastest in the last two years? What's your constraint to growing more?

## Section D — Why they're invisible (4 min)
15. Have you ever been written about, raised outside money, or done startup events? *(sanity-check obscurity)*
16. Was staying low-profile deliberate, or just never a priority?

## Section E — The invitation (5 min)
17. We'd like to feature you in our Hidden Champions profiles — here's the draft, you approve before anything goes out. Open to that?
18. We're building a network of operators like you — peer intros, occasional convenings, no fluff. Want in?
19. Who else should be on this list? *(referral — often the best new source)*
20. Best way and cadence to stay in touch?

## Close
- Restate next step + who owns it. Send the draft profile within 48h.
- Log outcome + update `status` in the tracker the same day.

## Logging cheat-sheet (tracker `status` field)
`identified → contacted → responded → interviewed → joined | declined`

## Consent & ethics
- Always get explicit OK before publishing a profile.
- Honour any "off the record" / "don't publish X" immediately.
- See [06-data-ethics-compliance](06-data-ethics-compliance.md).
